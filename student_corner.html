Regulations

The University has formulated the following guidelines,keeping in mind the current challanges faced by the Technical Educational System in the Country

  • The constituent college is free to make their own instruction,syllabus and schema of evaluation,while fulfilling the minimum acedmic standards.
    • The ACedmic year is divided into 3 segments
    • Two main semester
    • One supplimentary semester
      • Duration of each main semester will be 19 weeks and that of supplimentary semester will be weeks.
  • In main semester there is a provision for studentsto register for the courses underthe advice of councillors. This is expectedto ensure proper monitoring of students leadng to the improved learning capabilities and minimizing their chances of failure in the cources registered.
  • Credit System has been fallowing for the award of degree.
  • In credit system each cources is assigned with credit points. A student can take cources of credit weightage for meeting the requirementsof a dregree program. Such courceswith their grades and the credit earnedwill be included in the grade card issueed by the college at the end of each semester, this form the basis for determining student's academic performance in that semester
    • In Credit system students are assessed in two parts
    • Contineous Internal Evaluation(CIE)
    • Semester End Examination(SIE)
