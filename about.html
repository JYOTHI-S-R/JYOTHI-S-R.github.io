About us:

Vision

To craft professionally better-quality technocrats with ethically strong human values to cater local and global needs in the field of Computer Science & Engineering.

Mission

  • Provide congenial academic environment.
  • Prepare the students for taking up higher education.
  • Offer good training in the New technologies.
  • To facilitate continuous learning in the domain field with emphasis on professional ethics and minimizing the impact on environment.

Program Educational Objectives

  • PEO-01: Best in professional career, higher education by acquiring knowledge in mathematical, computing and engineering principles.
  • PEO-02: Analyze problems, design systems that are technically sound, economically feasible and socially acceptable.
  • PEO-03: Reveal ethical attitude, communication skills, exhibit team work in their profession and adopt to current trends by engaging in lifelong learning.

Program Outcomes

  • PO01: Foundation:Use the knowledge of computing, mathematics, science and engineering fundamentals to formulate, analyze, interpret data to solve engineering problems.
  • PO02: Curiosity: Aware of benefits of life-long learning, professional, ethical, legal, security, and social issues and responsibilities.
  • PO03: Societal Impact: Use and contribute information to the World Wide Web for self-development, and knowledge enhancement.
  • PO04: Modern Tool usage: Demonstrate with confidence, to acquire and use new and emerging engineering technologies, to solve global and local problems.
  • PO05: Engineer and Society: Ability to solve the problems with different approaches. Identify the required computing environment. Ability to segregate the results on best, worst and average case complexities.
  • PO06: Experimentation and Data Analysis: Able to use modern engineering software tools to analyze, design, test and generate the required report.
  • PO07: Professionalism: Capable to demonstrate managerial skill sets to solve problems in an intellectual way.
  • PO08: Environment and sustainability: Analyze and use different architectural models and able to build new models for different requirements with economic feasibility.
  • PO09: Communication skill: Able to work with team. Communicate effectively with team, clients, and at workplace.
  • PO10: Individual and team work: Promote collaborative learning and have Research orientation.
  • PO11: Current issues: Knowledge of contemporary issues.
  • PO12: Project Management & Finance: Recognition and development of Information Technology based solutions for day-to-day activities.
